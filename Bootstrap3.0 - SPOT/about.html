About Martin:

The biker from Uppsala, Sweden mostly rides Dirt and Park but occasionally also does some Free ride and 4x. Martin has been riding bikes for 8 years now. Back in 2007, he won the Nordic Championships in slopestyle. Shortly after this success a video showing his amazing street and slopestyle riding skills got posted on youtube and impressed people a lot. In 2008 Söderström had his international breakthrough. He rode at various dirt and slopestyle contests in Europe and proved that he is right among the world’s best riders.
